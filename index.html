Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the MakerInventory mobile application (herein referred to as "Application") created by Vertex Dev Labs as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

1. Information Collection and Use

The Application is designed as a local-first, offline tool. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information, inventory data, or application settings to external servers. All information added by you to the Application is saved locally on your mobile device using an SQLite database.

2. Device Permissions Required

To provide its core functions, the Application requires the following permissions:

  • Camera Permission: Used solely to scan QR codes and barcodes for inventory lookup and stock updates. The camera stream is analyzed locally in real-time on your device using Google's ML Kit API. No images, video recordings, or camera feeds are captured, saved, or uploaded to any servers.
  • Internet Permission: Used exclusively to fetch and display ads served by the Google AdMob SDK.

3. Third-Party Services and Advertisements

The Application utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Below are links to the privacy policies of the third-party service providers used by the Application:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

4. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Any changes will take effect immediately upon posting.
